Pricing Questions & Answers

When is payment due?

Payment is due upon completion of your move. We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards.

Are there any extra fees?

No hidden fees! Our rates include fuel, equipment, basic insurance, and taxes. Additional services are clearly itemized.

What about stairs or elevators?

Standard stairs and elevator access are included. Only extreme conditions (4+ flights, narrow stairs) may incur additional charges.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer military discounts, senior discounts, and off-peak pricing. Ask about current promotions.
