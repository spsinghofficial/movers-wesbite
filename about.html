Our Story

Founded in 2009, Elite Movers began with a simple mission: to take the stress out of moving. What started as a small family business with one truck has grown into your city's most trusted moving company, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Our founders, John and Maria Rodriguez, started Elite Movers after experiencing the frustration of unreliable moving companies during their own relocations. They knew there had to be a better way - one built on honesty, reliability, and genuine care for customers' belongings.

Today, we're proud to continue that family tradition of excellence, combining old-fashioned values with modern moving techniques and equipment.
