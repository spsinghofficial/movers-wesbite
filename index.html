Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our moving services

How much does it cost to hire Speedy Box Movers?

+

Our pricing depends on several factors including the size of your move, distance, time of year, and additional services. We offer competitive hourly rates starting at $120/hour for local moves with 2 movers and a truck. For accurate pricing, we provide free in-home estimates where we assess your specific needs and provide a detailed quote with no hidden fees.

How far in advance should I book my move?

+

We recommend booking your move at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving season (May-September) and at month-end. However, we understand that sometimes moves need to happen quickly, and we'll do our best to accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

Do you provide packing materials and services?

+

Yes! We offer comprehensive packing services and high-quality packing materials. Our services include full-service packing, partial packing, and packing supplies delivery. We use premium materials including sturdy boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and specialized containers for fragile items like artwork and electronics.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Absolutely! Speedy Box Movers is fully licensed and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and cargo protection to ensure your belongings are covered throughout the entire moving process. We're also members of professional moving associations and maintain all required certifications.

What areas do you serve?

+

We proudly serve the Greater Toronto Area and surrounding regions including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, and many other communities throughout Ontario. We also provide long-distance moving services across Canada.

How do you protect my furniture and belongings?

+

We take extra care to protect your belongings using professional moving blankets, shrink wrap, and padding. Our team is trained in proper lifting and loading techniques. We also offer furniture disassembly and reassembly services, and we have specialized equipment for handling pianos, artwork, and other valuable items.

Can you move specialty items like pianos or artwork?

+

Yes, we specialize in moving delicate and valuable items including pianos, artwork, antiques, electronics, and other specialty pieces. Our team has the expertise and specialized equipment needed to ensure these items are moved safely and securely.

Do you offer storage solutions?

+

Yes, we provide both short-term and long-term storage solutions in secure, climate-controlled facilities. This is perfect for situations where you need temporary storage between moves, downsizing, or when your new home isn't ready yet.

What happens if there's bad weather on moving day?

+

We monitor weather conditions closely and will contact you if severe weather might affect your move. While we move in most weather conditions with proper protection for your belongings, safety is our priority. If necessary, we'll work with you to reschedule to the next available date at no additional charge.

How do I prepare for moving day?

+

We'll provide you with a comprehensive moving checklist, but key steps include: decluttering and packing personal items, confirming parking arrangements, backing up computer files, defrosting refrigerators, and keeping important documents with you. We'll also discuss any special requirements during your pre-move consultation.
